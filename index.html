About Me

My name is Martha Wallace and I am a full-stack web developer. I have seven years working for Oil & Gas startups and have, over this tenure, created several simple apps to make sales, inventory, and accounting processes more efficient. I enjoy solving problems! In the past two years I have been able to hone my skills as a Salesforce Administrator as well as the admin for our instance of FieldFX (Liquid Frameworks software). I am someone who enjoys challenges, and enjoys the fact the no two days are the same – I love learning something new every single day. My experience with startups has given me three invaluable skillsets:

  1. I am a quick learner
  2. I am comfortable being uncomfortable (after all, that is an inherent feature of learning new skills)
  3. I like to fine tune processes to make them as efficient as possible

When I am not working on my programming skills, I enjoy travelling, competitive trivia, reading political or comedic biographies (Tine Fey’s Bossypants has become a yearly read), and trying beers in every city or country I visit – Iceland’s Surtur Nr. 47 became a dinner staple on my road trip to the land of Ice and Fire.
